About Juan

As an award winning advertising copywriter I worked for international brands like Pepsi, Bud Light and Burger King. I decided to change careers into software engineering because I rather solve problems than create them (sorry ad friends).

I like to create complete full stack apps with React for the front end and Node.JS with Express for the backend. Currently expanding my knowledge of databases and API development, digging deeper into GraphQL.

I'm currently living in New York where I sometimes perform Stand Up Comedy, but I'm originally from Uruguay (the other Paraguay).
